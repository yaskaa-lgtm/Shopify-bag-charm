## 1. FTC checklist (actionable, binary)

Rule on the Use of Consumer Reviews and Testimonials — **16 CFR Part 465**, effective 2024-10-21. Civil penalties up to **$53,088 per violation** (2025 inflation-adjusted amount; **no 2026 adjustment was made**, so this figure remains in force for 2026). Sources at bottom.

| # | Requirement | Pass criterion | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **No fake reviews** (consumer or AI-generated) | Zero reviews from anyone who didn't buy/use the product | ☐ |
| 2 | **No bought reviews** (positive or negative) | No payment/free-product-for-a-review-of-required-sentiment | ☐ |
| 3 | **Insider reviews disclosed** | Any review by employee/family/agent carries a clear disclosure | ☐ |
| 4 | **No incentive conditioned on sentiment** | Any review incentive is offered for a review of *any* rating and says so | ☐ |
| 5 | **UGC / "gifted" disclosure** | Creators posting seeded product use #ad or "gifted by Charmé" clearly & conspicuously | ☐ |
| 6 | **No review suppression** | Negative reviews are not hidden/deleted selectively | ☐ |
| 7 | **No fake social proof** | No fabricated follower/view/like counts or "1,247 viewing" widgets | ☐ |
| 8 | **Delivery transparency** | "8–15 business days" shown on PDP **and** at checkout | ☐ |
| 9 | **Credible compare-at** | $39 compare-at reflects a genuine reference price, not an invented anchor | ☐ **⚖️ LAWYER** |
| 10 | **No health/false claims, no misleading before/after** | No medical/benefit claims; imagery matches the real product | ☐ |

**Compare-at ($24.99 vs $39) — do this to stay credible:** the $39 must be a bona fide price the item is (or was) genuinely offered at, or a defensible reference (e.g., regular single-unit list price before promo). Do **not** set $39 purely to manufacture a discount. Keep dated evidence of the reference price. **⚖️ LAWYER: confirm your compare-at basis is defensible under FTC deceptive-pricing guidance and any applicable state law (e.g., CA).**

## 2. Plan to get REAL reviews (compliant)

1. **Judge.me automated request** (or Klaviyo Email 3 from B, not both) at **D+10** post-fulfillment. Wording asks for an **honest** review, offers **no reward**, conditions on nothing. ✅ Rule 4.
2. **Seeding to creators for UGC** — send free charms to micro-creators. They may keep it and post **only with clear disclosure** ("gifted by Charmé" / #ad) and **no obligation to be positive**. Track who received product. ✅ Rules 4 & 5.
3. **Compliant neutral incentive (optional):** if you offer a small discount for leaving a review, it must be given for a review of **any** sentiment, stated plainly ("Leave a review — positive or not — for 10% off your next order"), and the reviewer status disclosed if material. ✅ Rule 4. **⚖️ LAWYER: review the exact incentive wording.**
4. **Never** write reviews yourself, ask staff/family for undisclosed reviews, or filter out negatives. ✅ Rules 1–3, 6.
5. Seed the first reviews only from **real early buyers** — accept a slow start over a fake one.
